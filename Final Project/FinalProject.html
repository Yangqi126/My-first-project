The Syracuse International Film Festival was created in 2003

As a space where stories by, for, and about film and media can thrive, the Syracuse International Film Festival serves as a year-round platform for creative exchange and cultural connection. Through a wide range of screenings, talks, and special programs, members of the Festival community actively produce, present, and promote content that is designed not just to entertain, but to inspire, challenge, and bring people together.
Click Here.

Our Mission

Our mission is to engage, entertain, inform, and inspire the community by offering innovative and inclusive programs in art, fashion, film, music, and media. We also organize meaningful, entertainment-based special events throughout the year. Through our partnerships and thoughtfully curated programs, we aim to enrich the Central New York community by introducing them to the diverse world of international film.
